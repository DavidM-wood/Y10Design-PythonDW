The Evolution of the Hockey Helmet

The evolution of the hockey stick will start in the 1905 and go to 2020

  • 1905:

    - The first leather helmet was made but was used by few.
  • 1955:

    - The first plastic helmet was made in Sweden by Sven Johanssen.
  • 1962:

    - The first helmet was worn in an NHL game by Red Kelly of the Toronto Maple Leafs.
  • 1963:

    - The first helmet to cover the entire head was made by JOFA.
  • 1979:

    - 70% of NHLers were wearing helmets.

    - Helmets became mandatory for all new players. Players who were playing in the NHL before this rule were allowed to not wear a helmet. They were grandfathered in.
  • 1996:

    - The last player to not wear a helmet was Craig McTavish.
  • 2006:

    - All minor leagues made it mandatory to wear a visor.
  • 2013:

    - The NHL required all players with less than 26 games of experience to wear a visor.
  • Now:

    - Thousands of hours go into testing each helmet. New technology is being used each year.
