The Evolution of the Hockey Skates

The evolution of the hockey skates will start around 3000 BC and go till 2020

  • 3000 BC:

    - The first record of ice skates were from Finland 5 millenniums ago.
  • 1866:

    - The first skates made specifically for hockey.

    - The Starr hockey skates.

    - These skates were very flimsy and offered very little support. Modifications to this skate were made to make them more comfortable and offer more support.
  • 1927:

    - Bauer was born.

    - Now this company is one of the most popular brands in hockey.

    - They came up with a skate that offered more ankle support and a tendon guard.

    - These skates resemble modern skates.
  • 1980s:

    - Bauer was the first company to make skates out of plastic.

    - Before this, all skates were made of leather because of their ability to mold to the player's foot.
  • 2000:

    - Skates became lightweight and more durable with the introduction of carbon fibre.

    - Players would bake the skates in an oven to get a perfect fit around the heel and ankle.
  • Today:

    - Minor changes made to skates each year.

    - Most innovation in the past 10 years.
